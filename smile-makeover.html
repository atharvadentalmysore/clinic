Smile Makeover
in Mysore

Your smile is the first thing people notice. Whether it's closing a gap, brightening stained teeth, or completely redesigning your smile — we craft subtle, natural transformations you'll love.

What is a Smile Makeover?

Combining art and science to create your perfect smile. Our cosmetic dentistry approach focuses on subtle, natural-looking changes that enhance your unique facial features and boost your confidence. It may combine several cosmetic treatments to create a harmonious, natural-looking result that enhances your confidence.

Treatments We Offer

Porcelain Veneers & Laminates

Wafer-thin shells of medical-grade ceramic bonded to the front of your teeth. They instantly correct chips, cracks, gaps, minor misalignment, and severe discoloration. Custom colour-matched to your natural teeth for an undetectable result.

Composite Bonding

A cost-effective alternative to veneers for minor imperfections. Tooth-coloured resin is sculpted directly onto your teeth to repair chips, close small gaps, or reshape uneven edges. Completed in a single visit.

Professional Teeth Whitening

Clinical-grade whitening that delivers results far beyond what over-the-counter products can achieve. Safe, controlled, and effective — typically brightening teeth by several shades in one appointment.

Gum Contouring

If a "gummy smile" affects your confidence, minor reshaping of the gum line can dramatically change how your teeth are framed and displayed.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in a smile makeover?

A customized treatment plan that may include whitening, veneers, bonding, gum contouring, or alignment — depending on your goals and dental condition.

How long do porcelain veneers last?

High-quality porcelain veneers typically last 10–15 years with proper care. They are stain-resistant and maintain their natural appearance.

Is teeth whitening safe?

Professional whitening at a dental clinic is safe and effective. We use controlled concentrations that brighten teeth without damaging enamel.
