Teeth Cleaning & Scaling in Mysore

Professional teeth cleaning (scaling and polishing) is the most effective way to prevent gum disease and tooth loss. Even with regular brushing, plaque can harden into tartar that only a professional can remove safely.

At Atharva Dental, we use gentle ultrasonic scalers to remove stains and buildup, followed by a thorough polish to leave your teeth smooth and bright. It is widely considered the best preventive treatment for long-term health.

Health FAQ

Does scaling make teeth loose?

This is a common myth. Scaling actually removes the harmful tartar that causes bone and gum loss. Removing this buildup is essential to keep your teeth firm and healthy in the long run.

How long does a filling last?

Modern composite fillings typically last 7–10 years or more with good oral hygiene and regular checkups. We use premium grade materials to ensure maximum longevity.

What do I do if a tooth is knocked out?

Time is critical. Gently rinse the tooth in plain water (don't scrub!), place it back in the socket or keep it in a small container of milk, and reach our clinic within 30-60 minutes for the best chance of saving the tooth.
