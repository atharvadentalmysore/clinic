Why Straight Teeth Matter

Misaligned teeth create hard-to-clean pockets where bacteria accumulate, increasing the risk of cavities and gum disease. An improper bite can cause jaw pain, headaches, and uneven wear on your teeth over time. Orthodontic correction isn't just cosmetic — it's an investment in long-term oral and overall health.

Treatment Options at Atharva Dental

Traditional Metal Braces

The most proven and versatile orthodontic system. Modern metal braces are smaller, more comfortable, and more efficient than ever. They remain the gold standard for complex corrections including severe crowding, deep bites, and jaw misalignment.

Crooked teeth don't just affect your smile — they affect how you chew, speak, and care for your oral health. Our precision treatment options are designed for both teens and adults, focusing on both the aesthetics and the functional health of your bite.

How Orthodontic Treatment Works

  1. Consultation & Records: Dr. Kishore takes impressions, photographs, and X-rays to create a precise treatment plan.
  2. Treatment Plan: You'll see predicted results and receive a clear timeline before starting.
  3. Active Treatment: Regular adjustments (every 4–6 weeks) gradually move teeth into their ideal positions.
  4. Retention: After braces come off, retainers are worn to maintain the results long-term.

Braces for Adults

It's never too late to straighten your teeth. An increasing number of adults in their 30s, 40s, and beyond are seeking orthodontic treatment. With ceramic braces and clear aligners, treatment can be virtually invisible. Dr. Kishore has extensive experience treating adult patients who want to finally correct issues they've lived with for decades.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does braces treatment take?

12 to 24 months depending on complexity. Minor corrections may take as little as 6 months. Dr. Kishore provides a clear timeline after initial assessment.

Are clear aligners as effective as braces?

For mild to moderate misalignment, clear aligners deliver excellent results. For severe cases, traditional braces may be more effective.

Can adults get braces?

Absolutely. There is no age limit. Ceramic braces and clear aligners offer discreet options for working professionals.
